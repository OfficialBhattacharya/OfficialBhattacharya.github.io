States & Regions Explored

Delhi

The capital territory of India, a bustling metropolis that blends ancient history with modern development. Home to iconic landmarks like Red Fort, India Gate, and Lotus Temple.

West Bengal

The cultural heart of eastern India, known for its rich literary tradition, art, and the City of Joy - Kolkata. Bengali culture, Durga Puja, and intellectual heritage.

Maharashtra

The financial capital state, home to Mumbai - Bollywood's heart. Known for its progressive outlook, industrial development, and diverse landscapes from beaches to hill stations.

Kerala

God's Own Country - famous for backwaters, spice plantations, and high literacy rates. A tropical paradise with unique culture and Ayurvedic traditions.

Tamil Nadu

Ancient Dravidian culture, magnificent temples, and rich classical traditions. Home to Chennai and known for its technological advancement and cultural heritage.

Andhra Pradesh

The rice bowl of India, known for its spicy cuisine, classical dance forms, and the historic city of Hyderabad with its IT industry and Charminar.

Goa

India's smallest state, famous for its pristine beaches, Portuguese colonial architecture, vibrant nightlife, and unique Indo-Portuguese culture.

Himachal Pradesh

The abode of snow, nestled in the Himalayas. Famous for hill stations like Shimla and Manali, adventure sports, and pristine mountain landscapes.

Uttarakhand

Devbhoomi (Land of Gods), home to sacred sites like Rishikesh and Haridwar. Known for yoga, spirituality, and the source of holy rivers Ganga and Yamuna.

Meghalaya

The abode of clouds, known for its living root bridges, Cherrapunji (one of the wettest places on Earth), and unique matrilineal society.

Orissa

Land of Jagannath, home to the famous Puri Temple and Konark Sun Temple. Known for classical Odissi dance, handloom textiles, and rich cultural heritage.

Andaman & Nicobar

Tropical paradise islands in the Bay of Bengal, known for pristine beaches, coral reefs, rich marine life, and historical significance in India's freedom struggle.
