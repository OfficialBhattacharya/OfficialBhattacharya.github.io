UPSC News Digest

Issue II

March 8, 2025

AGRICULTURE

PM PRANAM Initiative

PM PRANAM incentivizes states to reduce chemical fertilizer usage through subsidy savings reinvestment, promoting sustainable agricultural practices and soil health across India.

GLOBAL HEALTH

Global Initiative on Digital Health

The Global Initiative on Digital Health (GIDH) under India's G20 Presidency creates WHO-managed digital health public infrastructure, revolutionizing healthcare accessibility worldwide.

GREEN ENERGY

National Green Hydrogen Mission

India's National Green Hydrogen Mission aims for 5 MMT annual production capacity by 2030, positioning India as a global green hydrogen hub.

SPACE TECH

Artemis Accords Signing

India's signing of Artemis Accords facilitates cooperation in NASA's lunar exploration program, marking a new era in space partnerships.

DIGITAL INDIA

Digital India Bhashini

Digital India Bhashini enables real-time digital content translation across all 22 scheduled Indian languages.

TRIBAL WELFARE

PM PVTG Development

Pradhan Mantri PVTG Development Mission targets socio-economic development of 75 Particularly Vulnerable Tribal Groups.

TRADITIONAL CRAFTS

PM Vishwakarma Scheme

PM Vishwakarma provides end-to-end support to 18 traditional crafts through subsidized loans and skill training.

YOUTH ENGAGEMENT

Mera Yuva Bharat

Mera Yuva Bharat (MY Bharat) establishes physical and digital infrastructure for youth volunteerism nationwide.

MARITIME HERITAGE

National Maritime Heritage Complex & Kochi Water Metro

The National Maritime Heritage Complex at Lothal includes the world's first tidal dock replica from Indus Valley Civilization. Meanwhile, Kochi Water Metro becomes the world's first integrated water transport system using electric hybrid boats.
