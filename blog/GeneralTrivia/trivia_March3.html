SPACE INNOVATION

Vikram-S: India's Private Space Revolution

Vikram-S, India's first privately developed launch vehicle, achieved suborbital flight in 2022, marking a historic milestone in India's commercial space sector and paving the way for private aerospace innovation.

GLOBAL RECOGNITION

International Year of Millets 2023

The International Year of Millets 2023 was proposed by India and adopted by the UN General Assembly, recognizing the nutritional and environmental benefits of these climate-resilient grains.

FUTURE TECH

Bharat 6G Vision Document

Bharat 6G Vision Document outlines India's roadmap for 6G standardization and IPR creation by 2030, positioning India as a leader in next-generation telecommunications.

DEVELOPMENT

Aspirational Blocks Programme

Aspirational Blocks Programme extends the Aspirational District model to 500 underdeveloped blocks nationwide, ensuring comprehensive grassroots development.

SPACE POLICY

Indian Space Policy 2023

Indian Space Policy 2023 permits non-government entities to establish launch pads and own satellites, revolutionizing India's space sector.

GEOPARKS

UNESCO Global Geoparks

India's UNESCO Global Geoparks include Kutch (Gujarat), Khangchendzonga (Sikkim), and Great Nicobar (Andaman).

WOMEN'S RIGHTS

Nari Shakti Vandan

Nari Shakti Vandan Adhiniyam mandates 33% reservation for women in Lok Sabha and State Legislative Assemblies.

CLIMATE SERVICES

Climate Services Framework

National Framework for Climate Services (NFCS) integrates meteorological data for sectoral climate resilience planning.

NATURAL FARMING

Natural Farming Mission

National Mission on Natural Farming promotes chemical-free agriculture through Bhartiya Prakritik Krishi Paddhati.
