GLOBAL CLIMATE

Loss and Damage Fund Established at COP28

The Loss and Damage Fund was formally established at COP28 to address climate impacts in vulnerable nations. This historic agreement marks a significant milestone in international climate finance, providing crucial support for countries facing irreversible climate damage.

INFRASTRUCTURE

PM Gati Shakti Master Plan

India's PM Gati Shakti National Master Plan integrates infrastructure planning across 16 ministries for enhanced multimodal connectivity and economic growth.

WATER MANAGEMENT

Ken-Betwa Link Initiative

The Ken-Betwa Link Project marks India's first inter-river linking initiative under the National Perspective Plan for water resource management.

QUANTUM TECH

National Quantum Mission

The National Quantum Mission targets developing intermediate-scale quantum computers with 50-1000 qubits by 2031, positioning India as a quantum technology leader.
