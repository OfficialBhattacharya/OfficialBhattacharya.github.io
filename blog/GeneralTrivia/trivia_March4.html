UPSC News Digest

Issue IV

March 22, 2025

BORDER DEVELOPMENT

Vibrant Villages Programme

Vibrant Villages Programme develops infrastructure in 2,967 border villages across 19 districts, strengthening border security and improving quality of life for frontier communities.

LOGISTICS REVOLUTION

National Logistics Policy

India's National Logistics Policy aims to reduce logistics costs from 14% to 8% of GDP by 2030, revolutionizing supply chain efficiency and boosting economic competitiveness through integrated infrastructure development.

SEMICONDUCTOR

India Semiconductor Mission

India Semiconductor Mission offers 50% fiscal support for establishing semiconductor fabs in India, positioning the nation as a global chip manufacturing hub.

ASSET MONETIZATION

National Monetisation Pipeline

National Monetisation Pipeline targets ₹6 lakh crore asset monetization in core infrastructure sectors (2022-25).

GEOSPATIAL

National Geospatial Policy

National Geospatial Policy deregulates mapping sector and establishes National Geospatial Data Registry for enhanced spatial data access.

GEOLOGY

Open Rock Museum

Hyderabad hosts India's first Open Rock Museum displaying 35 rock specimens from different tectonic plates.

MENTAL HEALTH

TeleMANAS Programme

National Tele Mental Health Programme (TeleMANAS) operates 51 centralized call centers across states/UTs.

CIRCULAR ECONOMY

India Plastics Pact

India Plastics Pact aims for 100% reusable/recyclable plastic packaging by 2030 through circular economy principles.

CULTURAL HERITAGE

Cultural Mapping & Dolphin Conservation

National Mission on Cultural Mapping documents cultural assets through a comprehensive digital database. Meanwhile, the National Dolphin Research Centre in Patna focuses on conservation of endangered Gangetic river dolphins.
