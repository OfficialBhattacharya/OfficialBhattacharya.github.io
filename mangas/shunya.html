About Shunya

In a world where reality and simulation blur, a lone observer witnesses the strange behavior of both humans and machines during a solar eclipse. This cyberpunk narrative explores themes of perception, consciousness, and the thin line between the digital and physical worlds.

Set in Mumbai, 2019, the story follows mysterious phenomena that challenge our understanding of reality itself. What begins as a simple observation of unusual behavior evolves into something far more profound and unsettling.
