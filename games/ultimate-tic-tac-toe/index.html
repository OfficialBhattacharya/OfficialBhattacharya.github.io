Ultimate Tic-Tac-Toe

A strategic twist on the classic game. Each small board's result determines where your opponent must play next!

How to Play

  • You are X, the computer is O
  • Your move in one board determines which board your opponent must play in next
  • If sent to a completed board, your opponent can choose any available board
  • Win 3 small boards in a row to win the game
