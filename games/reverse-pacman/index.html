Reverse Pacman

In this twist on the classic game, you are the hunter! Chase and eat the ghosts before time runs out.

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys or mobile controls to move your character
  • On mobile: Use the direction buttons or swipe gestures
  • Catch and eat all three ghosts before time runs out
  • Watch out for walls and obstacles
  • Choose your difficulty level to start
